Relio Soundscape Demo

Hello and welcome to the live Relio Soundscape Demo!

In this live demo you will hear when other people enter or leave the deemo and how busy it currenlty is in the demo. The sound related to how many people communicate has been simulated based on the amount of people.

For this demo, the sound is based on loops, meaning you might hear some artefacts from time to time.

For the best experience, we recommend headphones.
